# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Expected behavior

Use welcoming language, respect differing viewpoints, accept constructive feedback,
focus on what is best for the community, and show empathy toward others.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insults, sexualized language,
publishing private information, or other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers may remove,
edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for
behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
